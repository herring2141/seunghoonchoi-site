---
title: "Measurement"
subtitle: "Measure and compare to turn it into fact"
weight: 4
---
Two people can argue all day about which design is stronger until someone puts a number on it. Measurement is the work of measuring and comparing to turn a claim into a fact. One well-taken number often beats a hundred sentences of explanation, and even an answer from AI is only usable once it lines up with what was measured.
