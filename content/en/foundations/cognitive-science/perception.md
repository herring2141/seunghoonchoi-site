---
title: "Perception"
subtitle: "How the senses turn into a picture of the world"
weight: 1
---
When an AI fills in a blurry photo or reads a road sign, it is doing the same job your eyes do all day: turning raw signal into a usable picture. We do not take in the world exactly as it is. What we feel as reality is already trimmed and corrected by the brain, which is why two people can look at the same scene and honestly disagree about what was there.
