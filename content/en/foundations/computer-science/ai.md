---
title: "AI"
subtitle: "Letting a machine find the rules in data"
weight: 6
---
When a system predicts a material's property without anyone writing down the chemistry, it is letting the machine find the rules in data instead of spelling them out by hand. The approach is powerful, but in situations it has never seen it can be confidently wrong. So before you act on a result, you have to ask how far it has learned and where its knowledge runs out.
