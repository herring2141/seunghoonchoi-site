There is a long way between a good idea and a thing people actually use. Engineering is the work of closing that gap. For an AI model to move beyond a demo and become a product that runs every day, it too has to go through this stage.

## What it covers
- **Design** — deciding what to build and how.
- **Implementation** — actually building the design.
- **Operation** — keeping what you built running without stopping.
- **Quality** — making it work at the same level every time.
- **Safety** — the safeguards that keep it from harming people and the environment.
- **Productization** — turning something that works once into something many people can use.

## Where it leads
Within the limits [Physics](../physics/) has drawn, engineering turns the results of [Computer Science](../computer-science/) into real things. Questions of safety and responsibility meet [Law](../law/), and questions of cost and scale meet [Economics](../economics/).
