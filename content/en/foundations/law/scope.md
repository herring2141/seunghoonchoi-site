---
title: "Scope of Use"
subtitle: "The boundary of what you are allowed to do"
weight: 5
---
A model may be able to scrape a dataset or generate a likeness, but scope of use is the boundary that decides how much of that you are actually allowed to do. Being able to do something technically and being permitted to do it legally are two different matters. Miss that gap and a project that runs fine can still hit a line it was never allowed to cross.
