---
title: "Meaning"
subtitle: "Seeing how words and symbols come to stand for things"
weight: 3
---
Ask a system to handle the word "bank," and it has to work out whether you mean a riverbank or a place that holds money. That is the question of meaning: how words and symbols come to carry sense at all. The same word shifts meaning with the situation, and checking whether a machine actually caught that shift is what this work is about.
