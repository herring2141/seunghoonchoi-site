---
title: "Ethics"
subtitle: "Weighing what is allowed against what is not"
weight: 6
---
When a system trained on biased data starts handing out unfair decisions, "we could build it" stops being a good enough reason to ship it. Ethics is the standard for weighing what may be done against what must not. Even when something is technically possible, if it harms people or produces an unjust result, that is where it has to stop.
