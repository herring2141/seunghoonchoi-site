---
title: "Ontology"
subtitle: "Deciding what boxes to sort the world into"
weight: 5
---
Before you can store data, you have to decide what counts as a separate thing: is a "customer" the same box as an "account," or two different ones? That sorting is ontology, deciding which categories the world gets divided into and named by. The term sounds stiff, but the use is quite practical: how you split data into fields, and how you design a knowledge graph or a data model, all connect back here.
