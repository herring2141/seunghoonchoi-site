---
title: "Knowledge"
subtitle: "Checking how far the claim to 'know' actually holds"
weight: 2
---
When an AI answers a question in a confident tone, the worthwhile move is to ask how far that confidence is actually earned. That is the work of knowledge: testing where "knowing" really holds and where it does not. An AI can sound certain and still be only stringing plausible words together, which is a different thing from knowing.
