---
title: "Being"
subtitle: "Telling what actually exists from what is only talk"
weight: 1
---
When a model shows a number on screen, the useful first move is to ask whether it points to something real or is just a picture the model made up. That is what asking about being comes down to: separating what actually exists from what is only words. The same question helps you decide whether a measurement reflects an actual phenomenon or an artifact of the system that produced it.
