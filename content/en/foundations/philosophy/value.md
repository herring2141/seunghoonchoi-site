---
title: "Value"
subtitle: "The standard for deciding what comes first"
weight: 4
---
When a system can be tuned for speed or for safety but not fully for both, the facts alone will not tell you which to favor. That is where value comes in: the standard for judging what is good, what is bad, and what to put first. There are decisions the data cannot settle, and someone still has to choose what matters more.
